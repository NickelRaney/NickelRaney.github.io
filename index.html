Research interest

I am interested in computational neuroscience - applying mathematical tools to elucidate principles in neuroscience. My previous work mainly focused on using techniques in stochastic process and dynamical system to model Gamma oscillation. I also worked on mathematical models capturing the function of ipRGC neurons in lateral geniculate nucleus. Currently I'm working on a project focusing on the properties of a novel mean-field method.
